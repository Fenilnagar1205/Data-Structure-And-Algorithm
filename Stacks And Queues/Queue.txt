Queue — FIFO
Think of a queue at a shop:

You join at the back
You leave from the front
First person in = first person served

Enqueue A → [A]
Enqueue B → [A, B]
Enqueue C → [A, B, C]
Dequeue   → [B, C]      returns A  (first in, first out)
Dequeue   → [C]         returns B
